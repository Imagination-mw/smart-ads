User Help

Accounts
You can link your Smart-Ads account to social networking accounts on which you are registered on.
Navigate to the Accounts page and click on Add Account button to link the account.

You can update your linked SNS account by accessing the manage window of each account.

Similarly you can remove your account by accessing the same manage window.
Posts
You can view all your posts by navigating to the posts page.
Create a new post by clicking the new post button which will display a form for you to fill in the details.

You can also filter posts by using the filter options available to the left of the posts page.
